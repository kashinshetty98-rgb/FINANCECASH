Holdings

XIRR = annualised return (weighs every purchase/redemption by its date, then values the holding at the statement NAV date — same maths as Excel =XIRR()). Absolute = simple total gain, (market − invested) ÷ invested, not annualised. XIRR needs the CAS transaction history; funds held only a few weeks can show very large XIRR because a short period gets annualised. Live NAV pulls today’s official NAV & SEBI category from AMFI (amfiindia.com), matched by ISIN, and revalues each holding. VR ↗ opens the fund on Value Research to cross-check.
